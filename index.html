About

We are the the Mounds View chapter of NHS. NHS is a club for Juniors and Seniors that focuses on giving back to our community and school through service projects and volunteering. National Honor Society is an organization recognizing and promoting students who demonstrate strength of character, scholarship, service, and leadership, the four pillars of NHS. If you want to get in contact with us, check out our Executive Board and send one of us an email.
